Hi, I’m Agnes.

I’m part of the Online Software Engineering cohort starting on Jan 21.

I grew up in Staten Island, so I’m an original New Yorker depending on who you ask. I have since ditched the ferry rides to live in the trendier borough of Brooklyn.

I’m a creative people-person that loves math and science. In college, I was a Biology and Theatre double major and Math minor. For the past two years, I’ve worked as a clinical research coordinator at NYU Langone, where my responsibilities have spanned from collecting data on cardiac arrest patients to interviewing people about the health impacts of their socioeconomic circumstances.

In my spare time, I like to sing, cook food for my friends, and read books about why people and society do things. This blog will be dedicated to my thoughts and experiences as I attempt to juggle that, my job, and a software engineering bootcamp.

Thanks for reading!
